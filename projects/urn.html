Work / Urns

Urns

Urn is a series of wooden vessels made to be discrete, not ceremonial. They are designed to hold meaning without displaying it. Each piece is turned from solid oak and allowed to deform naturally over time, revealing tension, movement, and grain as part of the final form. Imperfection is not corrected. The vessels can hold precious objects, personal fragments or ashes, while keeping an organic, non-sacred shape: closer to a domestic object than a ritual one. Each urn is finished with a custom plexiglass lid, held securely by a turned internal rebate.
