Commission

Studioaprile accepts a limited number of commissions each season. Each piece is developed through a slow, considered process that respects the nature of the wood and the intent behind the object.

Commissions may begin from a specific brief or evolve from a loose conversation around use, scale, material and presence. The outcome is always a one-off piece, shaped by hand and allowed to retain the marks of its making.

What works well

  • One-off bowls and vessels
  • Urns, with clear constraints of size and intent
  • Small sets of related objects (typically 3–5 pieces)

Process

  1. Initial conversation to explore intent, context, and constraints
  2. Material selection and rough proposal
  3. Confirmation and production scheduling
  4. Making, with occasional progress updates
  5. Completion, review, and delivery

Natural movement, deformation and variation are integral to the work and are not corrected or disguised. Each commissioned piece will continue to evolve over time.

To enquire

Please email info@studioaprile.com with a short description of your idea, intended use and any material preferences.
