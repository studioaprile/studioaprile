Commissions

Studioaprile offers bespoke commissions, from singular statement wood turned pieces to curated collections for designers and brands. We also work with woodland owners to transform fallen trees into meaningful sculptural pieces, preserving their essence.

If you seek a handcrafted objects tailored to your space or your falle tree, we’d love to collaborate.

For commission inquiries: info@studioaprile.com

Our Process

  1. Discovery & Consultation: We explore your vision, materials, and context.
  2. Concept & Design: A proposal with sketches, finishes, and timeline.
  3. Booking & Scheduling: A deposit secures production time.
  4. Crafting & Progress: The piece is shaped by hand, with progress updates.
  5. Final Review & Approval: Images shared before final refinements.
  6. Delivery & Placement: Safe packaging with installation guidance if needed.

A commission with Studioaprile is more than a transaction, it’s a process of honoring material, memory and craftsmanship. Whether preserving the essence of a meaningful tree or creating a sculptural focal point, each piece is made with deep intention, allowing the wood’s own story to emerge.
